Welcome to my homepage! I am a 4th year Graduate student in Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley, California. My advisor is Prof. Ramamoorthi and I work in the Visual Computing Group. I'm currently an intern at Nvidia. I'm graduating in Spring 2015.

My primary research area is distributed rendering and I like to use signal processing ideas for filtering noisy signals in graphics, but broadly I'm interested in fast photo-realistic rendering. I hold a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science from IIT, Bombay, India. My brief resume can be downloaded here. For more information please follow the links above.
